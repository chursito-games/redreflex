About Red Reflex

Red Reflex is a simple reaction time test designed to measure how fast you can respond to a visual signal. When the screen turns red, your only goal is to tap or click as quickly as possible.

The idea is intentionally minimal. No accounts, no upgrades, no distractions. Just raw reaction speed. Each run consists of five rounds, encouraging you to improve your timing and beat your own personal best.

Why Red Reflex exists

Red Reflex was created as a lightweight, browser-based game that anyone can play instantly on desktop or mobile. It focuses on short sessions that are easy to repeat, making it perfect for quick challenges, casual competition with friends, or simply testing your reflexes.

Reaction time tests are commonly used in gaming, sports training, and cognitive exercises. Red Reflex offers a clean and accessible version of that concept, without unnecessary complexity.

Privacy & data

Red Reflex does not require registration and does not collect personal information such as names or email addresses. Some basic data may be stored locally in your browser (for example, your personal best score).

We may also use standard analytics tools to understand how the site is used and to improve performance. More details are available in our Privacy Policy.
