Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how Red Reflex handles information when you use the website.

Information we collect

Red Reflex does not require users to create accounts and does not collect personally identifiable information such as names, addresses, or phone numbers.

The game may store limited data locally in your browser (for example, reaction time scores or personal bests) to improve your experience. This data is stored using browser technologies such as localStorage and is not transmitted to our servers.

Analytics and advertising

We may use third-party analytics services to understand how visitors interact with the site. These services may collect anonymized information such as browser type, device, or approximate location.

In the future, Red Reflex may display advertisements provided by Google AdSense or similar services. These providers may use cookies or similar technologies to show relevant ads.

Third-party policies

Third-party services have their own privacy policies. We encourage users to review those policies for more information about how their data is handled.
